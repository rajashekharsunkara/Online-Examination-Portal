Examination Rules & Instructions

  1. You must stay in full-screen mode throughout the examination.
  2. Do not switch tabs, windows, or exit full-screen mode.
  3. You will receive warnings for violations. After 3 warnings, you will be automatically kicked out.
  4. All violations are logged and reported to the admin in real-time.
  5. Answer all questions carefully. You can navigate between questions.
  6. Click "Submit Exam" when you're done or when time runs out.
  7. Ensure stable internet connection throughout the exam.
  8. No external help or materials are allowed.
  9. Your exam session will be monitored continuously.
  10. Make sure you read all questions carefully before answering.
